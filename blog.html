    Food & Photography
    Food has always been an integral part of my life. From cooking family meals, baking cookies, and exploring new resturants, food has always a consistent source of happiness. Therefore, on January 2021, I started an Instagram food blog, @meghaneatsnc, to share the amazing food scene in the North Carolina Triangle. Through this venture, I have unexpectedly fallen in love with photography, marketing, and helping to support local businesses.
