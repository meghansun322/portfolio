About Me

Hello!

I’m Meghan Sun and a current second year student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I was born and raised in Chapel Hill, so I have been a Tar Heel my whole life. I’m currently pursuing a Computer Science major, Statistics and Analytics minor, and Business Administration minor. I have always love creating a product, whether it’s from baking, painting; so when I was introduced to the problem-solving and creative aspects of computer science, I instantly fell in love. Technology is such a powerful tool today and the potential it has to be used for a good has been one of my biggest insipration and motivator for my studies. I want to create products or application that will help make the world a better place. With an additional interest in marketing and food photography, I hope to find a career that combine these interests.


Outside of school, some of my favorite hobbies include trying new resturants, editing photos, going on long walks, trying new fitness classes, hiking, and making breakfast (yes, only breakfast a.k.a the best meal of the day).
